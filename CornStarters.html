Corn Starters Recipe | Corn Recipe| Easy & Quick Recipe

Corns are available in almost every seasons. these are generally quick, easy to make and more importantly lip-smacking in taste. it can be made with myriad options and one such popular and easy street food snack. Here I share three different variations of corn starters that can be enjoyed as snack or as munching food. With very less ingredients you can made such starters at home.

Ingredients

- Buttery Corn:

  • 1 Cup Boiled Corn
  • 1 Tbs Butter
  • Salt to Taste

- Masala Corn:

  • 1 Cup Boiled Corn
  • 1/2 Tbs Butter
  • 1/2 Tbs Red Chilli powder
  • 1/4 Tbs Jeera Powder
  • 1/2 Tbs Chat Masala
  • 1/2 Tbs Lemon Juice
  • Salt to Taste

- Corn Chat:

  • 1 Cup Boiled Corn
  • 1/2 Cup Boiled Green peas
  • 1/2 Cup Sprouted Grams
  • 1/4 Cup Chopped Onion
  • 2 Chopped Green Chillies
  • 1/2 Tbs Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 Tbs Chilli Flakes
  • Salt to Taste
  • Freshly chopped Coriander

Procedure

As corn can be available in markets in every season it become options for making different dishes out of it. Normally people like ti eat by roasting and putting some lemon and spices over it. But many other options are also there. I have shared such easy starters recipes here.

Buttery Corn:

It is way simple to make it. Boil the corn cobs in salty water. Once it get boiled separate the corns from cob. Put pan on flame add 2 Tbs of butter add boiled corns into it put some salt, you can also add seasonings like mixed herbs, oregano etc. And your Dish is ready.

Masala Corn:

It is easy to Make. Add 1 Tbs of butter in pan toss some boiled corns into it. Take out in bowl then add 1/2 Tbs of red chilli powder, 1/4 Tbs cumin powder, 1/2 Tbs chat masala, you can use dry mango powder instead. Then add some salt on it. And squeeze 1/2 lemon juice into it. Give it a good mix and you ready to eat this.

Corn Chat:

It is good habit to include salad in your diet. Corn chat can be a good option. For preparing it you need cup of boiled corn, 1/2 Cup of sprouted grams (Kala Chana), and 1/2 Cup of boiled green peas. Mix these together, then add 1/2 Finely chopped onion, 1 Chopped green chilli, 1 Tbs lemon juice, freshly chopped coriander and salt to taste. That’s it mix everything and enjoy the chat / salad.
