Momo’s | Dumpling | Dim sum | Indian veg Momo

Momo is a type of East and South Asian steamed filled dumpling. Momo is native to Nepal, Southwest Tibet, Bhutan as well as Indian Himalayan Region of Ladakh, Sikkim, Assam, and Arunachal Pradesh, and Darjeeling. It is popular across a wider region of the Indian subcontinent. Momo’s are extremely popular among the natives of the Indian subcontinent and can be found in every kind of shop from restaurants to street vendors.

In this recipe, the savoury veggie filling has a balanced taste and is lightly spiced, so that it accompanies any spicy and hot sauce very well. Momo are traditionally steamed but can also be deep fried or pan fried. Here I shared both methods of preparing it.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbs Chopped garlic
  • 1 Tbs Chopped ginger
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup grated cabbage
  • 1/2 cup grated carrot
  • 1/2 Tbs black pepper powder
  • 1 cup plain flour
  • Salt to taste
  • Water
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil

Procedure

Veg Momo’s are simple to prepare. You need to grate veggies like cabbage, carrots. So, it can easily get stuffed in sheets.
Let’s make dough for Momo’s. First take 1 cup of plain flour (All-purpose flour), add 1/4 Tbs salt to it. Then add water in batches and knead a medium-soft dough. Knead well, and cover with damn cloth. Keep aside to rest for 10 minutes.

Now we make stuffing of veg momos. For this Put 3 Tbs of oil in pan, add 1 Tbs grated ginger and 6-7 Finely chopped garlic into it. Stir fry for minute. Then add 1 finely chopped onion. Cook for minute, then add 1 Cup of grated cabbage, ½ Cup grated carrot. Mix well. Then add salt to taste. And 1/2 Tbs black pepper powder. Get all mixture together.

Again, knead the dough for 2-3minutes. Take small portions of dough and roll them to a thin round sheet. Take 1 sheet add 1 Tbs of prepared stuffing into it. Now take one end of sheet and fold one end over other. Repeat procedure util you reach half sheet. Then put remaining half sheet over that fold. Then press gently. And you half-moon shaped Momo is ready. You can give any shape as per your preference. It required some practice to make perfect shape of Momo.

Once all momo’s get ready. Put some water in vessel or steamer. Arrange momo’s on plate and steam them for 10 minutes. steam momos for 10-12 minutes or till shiny sheen appears over it. It can be healthy option if you avoid oily food. But you can fry momos, after preparing them. Heat oil in pan, deep fry them until they turn crispy and golden in colour.

Serve moms with some sauces or chutney. And your deep fried momos are ready to serve.
