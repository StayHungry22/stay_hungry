Thandai Recipe | Hoil Special drink recipe

Thandai is an Indian cold drink prepared with a mixture of almonds, fennel seeds, watermelon kernels, rose petals or gulkand, pepper, poppy seeds, cardamom, saffron, milk and sugar. It is native to India and is often associated with the Maha-Shivaratri and Holi or Holla mahalla festival. For Holi, bhang (the leaves and flower heads of cannabis) in little amount is added to Thandai but my version is minus the bhang since this is what we could drink when we were kids.

Thandai is a safe drink to have. It is basically a cooling drink. It is also very nutritious as it is a blend of protein-rich milk and dry fruits.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup almonds
  • 1/4 Cup pistachio
  • 1/4 Cup cashew
  • 1/4 Cup melon seeds
  • 2 Tbs poppy seeds
  • 1/2 Tbs black pepper
  • 8-10 cardamom pods
  • 10-15 saffron strand
  • 1 Tbs Fennel seeds
  • 1 Cup water
  • 2 Tbs rose petals jam
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 Cups Milk

Procedure

As It is refreshing drink you can refer it in summers also. It is quite easy t o made. Here I shared a recipe of thandai that can lasts for a month, if you stored in proper manner.

For preparing thandai you need 1/4 Cup almonds, 1/4 Cup pistachio, 1/4 Cup cashew, 1/4 Cup melon seeds, 2 Tbs poppy seeds, 1/2 Tbs black pepper, 8-10 cardamom pods, 10-15 saffron strand and 1 Tbs Fennel seeds. Put them together in bowl, add 1 Cup of water to it. And soak for 7-8 hours. After soaking, transfer all soaked nuts to grinding jar. Grind it to fine paste. Then add 2 Tbs of rose petal jam, also called as “Gulkand” in Marathi and in hindi language. Again, grind the mixture.

Now You can use this Thandai masala, as it. But if you want to store this for later then, make sure that you will do this step. Take 1 cup of sugar in pan. Add 3/4 cup of water into it. Let the sugar melts, then add prepared thandai masala into syrup. Now cook it for 10-15 minutes on low flame. It gets thickened after some time. Once it gets thicker, switch off the flame, and let the mixture cools down. After cooling store this thandai masala in packed container. Keep this container in freeze so it lasts for month. Now to make thandai milk from masala, take 2-3 Tbs of thandai masala in glass, then add Cold milk to it. This is how you can make such a refreshing milk on Holi festival.
