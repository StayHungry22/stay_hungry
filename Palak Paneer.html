Easy Palak paneer Recipe | Spinach Recipe | Spinach with Indian Cottage Cheese

Palak paneer is a vegetarian dish originating from India, consisting of paneer also called as Cottage Cheese simmered in a smooth creamy paste made from pureed spinach, called palak in Hindi, Marathi, and other Indian languages. Palak Paneer is most loved north Indian dishes that's eaten with roti, Paratha, Garlic Naan, Jeera Rice or even with plain Basmati rice. It is very easy to prepare and can be made in so many ways. This is easy and healthy palak paneer recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Spinach
  • 250 Gm Paneer
  • 2-3 Ice Cubes
  • 5-6 Green Chillies
  • 1 piece of ginger
  • 7-8 Garlic cloves
  • 3 Tbs Oil
  • Pinch of Asafoetida (Hing)
  • 1/2 Tbs Jeera
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 Tbs Fresh Malai / Cream

Procedure

Palak paneer is served hot with a side such as roti, naan, parathas, makki di roti, or boiled rice. It can also be served with onions on the side for a more traditional approach. Palak paneer is prepared by first boiling and pureeing spinach. The puree is then mixed with, Grilled cubes of paneer. Palak paneer is typically spiced with ginger, garlic, garam masala, turmeric, chili powder and cumin.

First boil some water and add 1/2 Tbs salt into it. Put fresh spinach leaves into water. And boil it for 5-8 Minutes. Once spinach get boil drain the water and let the spinach cool down slightly. Then put boiled spinach into grinding jar along with add 2 Green chilli, half inch of ginger and 4-5 Garlic clove. Add 2-3 Ice cubes into jar so that spinach puree retain their colour. It gives really good aroma and taste to palak panner. Grind the paste finely.
Take 250 Gms of Panner also called as cottage cheese, cut into square pieces. Add some oil in pan and shallow fry the paneer cubes from both sides. It turns golden in colour. Now To prepare Palak paneer, put 2-3 Tbs oil in pan, add 1/2 Tbs cumin seeds and pinch of hing. Once it gets popped add chopped garlic into it. Put spinach puree into pan and cook for 4-5 Minutes on medium flame. Add fried paneer cubes into it. Give a mix. Adjust salt according to taste. Once it gets cooked completely drizzle 1 Tbs of fresh malai on top of it. And Palak panner is ready to serve.
