Pinwheel Recipe | Aaloo stuffed Rolls | Aaloo Bhakarvadi

A unique and attractive way of preparing the traditional aloo samosa recipe which can be best party starters or just for the evening light snack. This is an ideal snack for those who find preparing or shaping samosas inconvenient and yet would like to relish it. There are several names to this unique samosa recipe and one such popular names is samosa rolls.

Unlike the traditional samosa’s, samosa roll is rolled with potato stuffing which is later sliced to individual pinwheels. These individual pinwheels would have exposure to aaloo stuffing and are shallow fried till crisp.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Wheat flour
  • 3 Tbs Rice flour
  • 4-5 Boiled potatoes
  • 1/2 Cup Boiled green peas / frozen peas
  • 1 ginger piece
  • 8-9 Garlic cloves
  • 5-6 Green chillies
  • Coriander
  • 1/4 Tbs Hing (Asafoetida)
  • 1/2 Tbs cumin
  • 1/2 Tbs salt
  • Oil to Fry

Procedure

Pinwheel is kind of samosa version, and it is way simple to prepare. For preparing it, take 1 Cup of wheat flour, add 2-3 Tbs of rice flour. Rice flour is added in wheat flour to make outer layer crispier. Mix both flour together, put 1/4 Tbs salt into flour. Now add water as needed to knead dough. Knead dough as usually we knead for roti’s. Once it gets done. We start making the inner filling of pinwheels.
Take 4-5 Boiled potatoes, chopped them into small pieces by using knife. Now put 1 piece of ginger around an inch, 8-9 garlic cloves, 5-6 green chillies, and bunch of coriander. Grind to fine paste. Put 3 Tbs oil in pan. Add 1/2 Tbs cumin, 1/4 Tbs Hing. Fry for a minute. Add Prepared ginger-garlic-chilli paste into oil. Cook for minute again, then add chopped boiled potatoes and 1/2 cup boiled green peas. Mix altogether nicely. Mash some potatoes while cooking. Add salt to taste. Cook the stuffing for 3-4 minutes in total. Then keep aside for cooling down.
Start kneading dough again. Take small portion of dough and roll it to normal size roti, then take 2 Tbs of aaloo stuffing and put it on roti. Take spoon or spatula and spread stuffing over roti. Once stuffing gets spread, start rolling roti from one end to other. It will turn into complete roll of roti. Now take knife and start partitioning roti into small pieces. Make 8-10 pieces and separate it. Now take one piece of roll, put some pressure on one side, so it gets flattened. Follow the same procedure for rest.
Now put some oil in pan, and shallow fry all pinwheels / aaloo samosas in batches. Cook each side for at least 2 minutes on low flame. So, it turns crispy and golden in colour. You can serve it hot with some green chutney, or with ketchup. And you are ready to enjoy your unique stuffed pinwheels.
