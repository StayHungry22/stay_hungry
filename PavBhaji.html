Pav Bhaji Recipe | Street style Pav Bhaji | Instant Pav Bhaji Recipe

Pav Bhaji is one the Favourite street style food of teenagers. It is fast food dish from India. It Consists of thick vegetables curry served with soft bread or pav. It is good way to add more and more vegetables to your plate. Vegetables in the curry may commonly include potatoes, onions, carrots, chillies, peas, bell peppers and tomatoes.

Street sellers usually cook the curry on a flat griddle (tava) and serve the dish hot. A soft white bread roll is the usual accompaniment to the curry, but this does not preclude the use of other bread varieties such as chapati, roti or brown bread. Pav Bhaji having its different spicy tangy and buttery flavour which insists you to eat it again and again. It is very easy to make at home.

Ingredients

- Pav Bhaji:

  • 2 Tbs oil
  • 1/2 Tbs Jeera
  • Pav Bhaji masala
  • 2 Chopped Tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup Cauliflower
  • 1 Chopped Beetroot
  • 2 Chopped potatoes
  • 1/2 cup Green peas
  • 1.5 cup Water
  • 2-3 Cubs of butter
  • 3 Chopped Onions
  • 2 Chopped Capsicums
  • 1 Tbs Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1/4 Tbs Turmeric
  • 1 Tbs Red chilli powder
  • Salt to Taste
  • Half lemon
  • Coriander for garnishing

- For Masala Pav:

  • Pav
  • Butter
  • Coriander for garnishing

Procedure

Pav Bhaji is a type of street food, loved by food lovers. It consists of varities of vegetables, lot of butter. And this thick gravy (Bhaji) is eaten with ladi pav / Bread. There are many ways to prepare it. I show the simplest one here. Put 2-3 Tbs of oil in pressure cooker, then add 1/2 Tbs of cumin, 1 Tbs Pav bhaji masala, once it gets fry add 2 Chopped tomatoes,1/2 Cup cauliflower, 1 Chopped beetroot, 2 Chopped potatoes and a cup of green peas. Combine all veggies together with masala, then add 1-1.5 cup of water and then close lid of cooker. Cook for 4 whistles.

Let cool down the pressure cooker, open lid and mash veggies by using spatula or masher. Now add 2-3 Tbs of butter in pan then add 3 finely chopped onions and cook them for 2 minutes. Then add 2 chopped capsicums and add 1 Tbs ginger-garlic paste into it. Then add dry spices like 1 Tbs of red chilli powder, 1/2 Tbs turmeric, 1.5 Tbs of pav Bhaji masala, and salt to taste. Mix all spices nicely then add 1 Tbs lemon juice into it. Give it a good mix then add prepared veggie mixture into pan. And now to get a perfect gravy texture use masher and mash all together. Once it done add freshly chopped coriander on top of it. And your street style pav bhaji is ready to serve.

You can serve it with pav, or even you can toast the breads, pav with little butter on it. Even you can make masala pav out of it, just you have to put some butter in pan, a tablespoon of bhaji and roll the breads or pav on it. It became easy to eat.
