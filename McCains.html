Potato Bites | Aaloo Snack | Aaloo Bites | Chilly potato Bites

Aaloo bites | potato bites | chilly potato bites is one of the side snack, muching recipe. It is served as side dish but anyone can have it as it is. Many potato based dishes are loved by non-vegetarian peoples, because potato replaced meat tikkis, bites, with several of its versions. Learn how to make Potato Bites or homemade McCains with ingredients that are easily available at home. Potato Bites is one of the most loved snack of everyone. It’s quick and easy to make with ingredients that are available at home.
Everyone loved snacks. It is light, crunchy, junk food. So here I show how to make it step by step without doing any mess.

Ingredients

  • 3 Potatoes
  • 1 cup Rice Flour
  • 1 Tbs Chopped Garlic
  • 1 Tbs Chili Flakes
  • 1 Tbs Salt
  • 1/2 Tbs Black pepper
  • Oil

Procedure

All you need some potatoes, cup of Rice flour, salt, pepper, chili flakes and chopped garlics.

First you need to boil 4-5 potatoes. To make it quickly, I cook potatoes in pressure cooker for 2-3 whistles. Let the cooker cool down. Now take pan add 1/2 cup of water. Let it boil. Add 1/2 Tbs of salt, 1/4 th spoon of black pepper powder, mixed-herbs, and chili flakes.

Once it gets boiled add rice flour into it. Mix the batter and cook it for 2-3 minutes. Take out flour mixture in bowl. Now grate the boiled potatoes into mixture of flour and make a dough out of it. Roll the dough into medium sized cylindrical shape and cut it down into bite pieces.
Heat oil in deep pan. Once oil heated add potato bites into oil. Fry uniformly for 3-4 minutes. They turn golden and crispy. Your potato bites are ready to serve.
