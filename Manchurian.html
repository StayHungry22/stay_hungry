Manchurian | pakoda | gobipakoda | Machurian balls Recipe

Manchurian is an Indian Chinese fried cabbage dish. Veg Manchurian is dish made with wisps of vegetables formed into dumplings and dunked into a sauce that explodes with hot, sweet, sour, and salty flavours. There are two different variants of Manchurian, dry and with gravy. Both variants are prepared by using common ingredients like cabbage, corn flour, Maida flour, spring onion, soy sauce, chili sauce, minced garlic, etc. and has typical garnish of spring onion.

Ingredients

-For Manchurian Balls:

  • 1 Grated Cabbage
  • 1/4 Cup All-purpose flour
  • 1/4 Cup Corn flour
  • 1 Tbs Ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 Tbs soya sauce
  • 1 Tbs vinegar
  • Salt to taste
  • Food colour(Optional)

-For Schezwan Chutney

  • 7-8 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Inch Ginger piece
  • 6-7 Green chillies
  • 1 Tbs soya sauce
  • 1 Tbs vinegar
  • 1 Tbs corn flour
  • Salt to taste
  • Food colour

Procedure

As our young generation are more attracted towards fast foods, Indo-Chinese foods can be good option for them. It takes less time to prepare. Here I show how I usually made Manchurian balls, or cabbage pakora at home.

Take 1 Finely grated cabbage, add 2-3 Tbs of all-purpose flour, 2-3 Tbs of Corn flour, salt to taste, pinch of food colour (Optional). Now mix altogether nicely. Don’t add water in the mixture. Cabbage releases its moisture, so the flour gets enough water for binding. Mix everything and keep it aside to rest for 10 minutes. After 10 minutes mix the mixture and start making balls of it. You can add other veggies like carrots, spring onion, etc. Here I used only cabbage.

Put some oil in pan to fry. Heat oil on medium flame. Once oil gets heated add prepared cabbage balls. Fry uniformly on both sides, cook at least 3-4 minutes on low flame. Once it gets crispy layer and golden in colour take them out in plate.

For preparing simple and quick schezwan sauce, take 1-inch ginger piece, 5-7 Garlic cloves, 7-8 green chillies. Grind them in grinder finely. Now put 2 Tbs oil in pan, add ginger-chilli-garlic paste to it. Fry for 2-3 minutes then add 1/2 cup water. Mix well. Then add salt to taste, 1 Tbs soya sauce, 1/2 Tbs vinegar, and pinch of food colour. Give it a good mix. Then cook it for 4-5 minutes. Now take 1 Tbs corn flour in bowl. Add 3 Tbs water to it. Mix well and add it to chutney. So, it gets thickened.

Now serve the cabbage pakora / Manchurian with homemade simple schezwan sauce.
