Dal Tadka | Dal Recipe | Lentils Recipe

Dal takda is popular dish served in Indian restaurants made with Moong dal, Toor dal( or Arhar dal). Basically da; tadka is cooked lentils which are tempered with oil fried spices and herbs This dals and ingredients are found in every Indian kicken very easily. Also dal is important part of our Indian food. There are lots of version available to make this.This recipe can be served as a side to roti or rice.

Ingredients

- For Marination:

  • 1/2 cup Moong dal
  • 1/2 cup Toor dal (Arhar dal)
  • 4 Tbs oil
  • 1/2 Tbs Jeera
  • 1 Tbs Mustard seeds
  • 4-5 Curry leaves
  • 2 Chilies
  • 1/4 Tbs Hing (Asafetida)
  • 1 Onion
  • 1/2 Tbs Ginger-garlic paste
  • 4-5 Garlic cloves
  • 1/2 Tomato
  • 1 Tbs Red chilli powder
  • 1/2 Tbs Tumeric
  • 1/2 Tbs Garam Masala
  • 1/4 Tbs Jeera Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 Red chillies
  • 1/4 Kashmiri red chilli powder
  • Coriander

Procedure

Most of the restaurants serves dal tadka along with naan, rotis, and rice. This can be a good option for vegetarian peoples. It is rich, thick and nutritious dish. It may have some variations depending on which lentils are used while making it. Here I shared an easy option for preparing it. I used moong (Mung Bean) and tur dal (Pigeon pea).
First take both the lentils in equal ratio. Wash properly. Chopped onion and tomato finely. Add 2-3 Tbs of oil in pressure cooker, put pinch of Asafoetida (Hing), 5-6 curry leaves, 2 slitted green chillies, 1/2 Tbs of cumin, and mustard seed. Once they get cracked add chopped tomatoes and onions into it. Add spoon of ginger-garlic paste. Roast for a minute. Then add spices like red chili powder, turmeric, half spoon of garam masala, salt to taste. Give a good mix then add some coriander into it. Once add bind properly put our lentils into it. Close the lid of pressure cooker and cook for 3-4 whistles. Let cooker cools down. Take out prepared dal into bowl. Now heat tadka pan, add some oil into it, put mustard seeds, and cumin to it. Add 2-3 Kashmiri chillies into oil. Add 1/4th Tbs of red chilli powder for rich color. Once tadka get ready add it into dal. Granish with freshly chopped coriander.
Your hotel style dal tadka is ready. Give it a Try. You will definitely like this.
