Paneer Masala | Cottage cheese Recipe | Easy recipe

An extremely popular and tasty way to prepare a rich and creamy paneer recipe with spices and yoghurt. Paneer based curry has a cult across India and dhaba style cooking has only infused a new and rich way to prepare it.
Paneer masala is an Indian dish of marinated paneer cheese served in a spiced gravy. It is a vegetarian alternative to chicken masala. It is easy recipe which turn best after making. It is made with Paneer Cubes, Cubes of Onions, and Capsicum. It is full of vegetables you can add your Favourite veggies like corn, Bell peppers, etc.

To prepare Paneer Masala all you need is Cup of curd, some spices like Red chilli powder, turmeric, Garam masala, jeera powder, Dry Mango Powder( Amchur ) powder also called as dry mango powder, pinch of black pepper, roasted gram flour ( besan ), salt to taste, Pinch of red food color for getting hotel style look to your dish. Add Ginger garlic paste to make dish flavorful.

Ingredients

- For Marination:

  • 3/4 th Cup Curd
  • 1 Tbs Red chilli powder
  • 1/2 Tbs Turmeric
  • 1/2 Tbs Jeera powder
  • 1/4 Tbs Garam masala
  • 1/4 Tbs Amchur powder
  • 1 Tbs Salt
  • 1 Tbs Ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 Tbs Gram flour
  • 1/2 Tbs Kasoori methi
  • 2 Slited green chillies
  • Pinch of Food Colour (Optional)

- For Preparation:

  • 2 Tbs oil
  • 1/2 Tbs Jeera (Cumin Seeds)
  • 1/4 Tbs Hing (Asafetida)
  • 1/4 Tbs Ginger-garlic paste
  • Freshly chopped Coriander

Procedure

First add a Cup of curd, some spices like Red chilli powder, turmeric, Garam masala, jeera powder, Amchur powder also called as dry mango powder, pinch of black pepper, roasted gram flour ( besan ), salt to taste, Pinch of red food color and Ginger garlic paste ,together in a bowl and then add paneer cubes, chopped onions and capsicum into it. Coat well. And Keep it for 1-2 hours. After the marination complete add 3 Tbs Oil in Pan add jeera or cumin seeds then add marinated mixture cook for 5-10 minutes on low flame. Garnish it with freshly chopped coriander and serve it.
