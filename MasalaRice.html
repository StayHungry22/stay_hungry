Masala rice | Vegetable spicy rice | Spicy rice recipe | Pulao

Masala rice is a flavoured and spicy rice recipe prepared with dry spices and vegetables. It is a one-pot meal and hence an ideal lunch box recipe and easily appreciated by kids and all age groups. The rice is infused with spices and masala with choice of veggies and hence served by itself or tastes great with raita recipes.

Cooking masala rice is very simple without any complicated steps in it. basically, the recipe is very similar to the traditional pulao recipe prepared across India. but the spiced rice may contain more spices and flavours compared to the pulao recipes. but obviously, it completely depends upon the type of pulao or pilaf recipe from a particular region is compared. the pulao recipes have fewer spices and contain a hero ingredient like pudina, coriander or methi, but each region has its own way of cooking pulao and may differ. moreover, in this recipe, I have used basic spices available in every kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbs Oil
  • 1 Tbs Mustard seed
  • 1 Tbs cumin seed
  • 1 Slitted chilly
  • 5-6 curry leaves
  • Pinch of Hing (Asafoetida)
  • 1 Chopped onion
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • 1 Tbs ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup green peas
  • 2 Tbs chopped carrot
  • 2 Tbs snake beans
  • 2 Tbs Capsicum
  • 1 Tbs red chilli powder
  • 1/2 Tbs garam masala
  • 1/2 Tbs turmeric
  • 1 Cup prepared rice
  • Coriander

Procedure

Masala rice is quite easy to prepare. You need cooked rice, some veggies, and dry spices like chilli powder, turmeric, garam masala, which are usually found in everyone’s kitchen.

First put pan or kadhai on flame, add2-3 Tbs of oil, heat oil. Then add 1 Tbs of cumin seeds, and mustard seeds to it. Let them pop. Then add 5-6 curry leaves, 2 Slitted green chillies, 1/4 Tbs Hing. Mix it. Then add 1 finely chopped onion. Cook for a minute. Then add 1 Chopped tomato. Add salt to taste. Salt helps to soften tomatoes quickly. Once it gets soften, add 1 Tbs ginger-garlic paste. It enhances the taste of rice. Then add other veggies like, 1/4 cup green peas, 2 Tbs chopped carrot, 2 Tbs chopped snake beans, 1/2 Chopped Capsicum. Stir fry the veggies for a minute. Then add 1/4 Cup water. And close the lid of pan. Cook veggies on medium flame.

Take lid out after 2 minutes then give it good mix and add dry spices like 1 Tbs red chilli powder, 1/2 Tbs garam masala, 1/2 Tbs turmeric. Mix altogether. Add a cup of cooked rice to pan. Mix all ingredients together. And yeah, your masala rice is ready. Add freshly chopped coriander while serving.
