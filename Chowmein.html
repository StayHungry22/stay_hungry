Chowmein Noodles | Chinese Food | Street style veg chowmein Recipe

Chow Mein are Chinese stir-fried noodles with vegetables and sometimes meat or tofu; the name is a romanization of the Taishanese chow mein. The dish is popular throughout the Chinese diaspora and appears on the menus of most Chinese restaurants. It is particularly popular in India, Nepal, the UK, and the US.

A classical lip-smacking Indian street food recipe made with thin noodles tossed with sliced veggies. it is basically Indian version or indo-chinese cuisine recipe with a lot of ingredients and style inherited from Asian cuisine. it is an ideal party starter or an appetizer recipe that can be shared by itself without any sides but tastes great with any Manchurian gravy.

Ingredients

  • 2 Noodles cakes
  • 2 Cup Water
  • 1 Tbs corn flour
  • 1 Tbs red chilli powder
  • 1/2 Tbs cumin powder
  • 1 Tbs garam masala
  • 1 Tbs sugar
  • 3/4 Tbs Salt
  • 1/2 Tbs black pepper
  • 2 Tbs vinegar
  • 2 Tbs soya sauce
  • 4-5 Tbs oil
  • 1/2 Inch Sliced ginger
  • 7-8 Chopped garlic cloves
  • 1 Sliced onion
  • 1/2 sliced capsicum
  • 1/2 Sliced carrot
  • 1/4 Cup spring onion
  • 1/4 Cup sliced cabbage

Procedure

As chowmein contains veggies, the first task is to be chopped them lengthwise, and finely. So, it gets easier while cooking them. Chopped 1 onion, 1 Capsicum, 1 Carrot, Spring onions, and half cabbage. Chopped them finely. Now take a deep vessel or pan, add 2-3 Cups of water, and 1/2 Tbs salt to it. Let water boils. Add 1 Tbs of oil, so noodles do not get stick to each other. Then add noodle cakes into it. Boil noodles for 4-5 minutes. Then drain water from it.

For preparing chow mein noodles at home, you can use readymade noodles masala or can follow the steps. Take one bowl add 1 Tbs corn flour, 1 Tbs red chilli powder, 1/2 Tbs cumin powder, 1 Tbs garam masala, 1 Tbs sugar, 3/4 Tbs Salt, 1/2 Tbs black pepper powder, 2 Tbs vinegar, 2 Tbs soya sauce, 4-5 Tbs oil. mix them altogether. Your homemade noodle masala is ready.

Now put large pan, or kadhai, Add 3-4 Tbs oil to it. Heat oil, then add 1/2 Inch Sliced ginger, 7-8 Chopped garlic cloves. Stir fry for a minute. Then add all chopped veggies to it. Stir fry them on high flame. Once they get cooked. Add prepared noodle masala to it. Then add boiled Noodles to it. Mix them nicely. Add spring onion on top. Cook for 2 minutes, once noodles get coated with masala. Switch of the flame. And Serve the chowmein noodle. You can serve it with some schezwan chutney, and some fried noodles.
