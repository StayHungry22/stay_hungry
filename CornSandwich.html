Corn-Mayo Sandwich | Sandwich Recipe | Creamy Sandwich

It’s easy Sweet Corn Sandwich recipe. Perfect for school lunch boxes and can be made in less than 15 minutes.Make this ultimate veg mayo sandwich with crisp toasted bread, crunchy veggies, creamy mayo and spices to flavour up. This recipe makes the best breakfast sandwich that is super-fast to make on a busy morning. Mayonnaise sandwiches are most commonly served in eateries, cafes and fast food chains. These are made in numerous ways and tagged with different names.

Ingredients

  • 1 Chopped onion
  • 1 Chopped Capsicum
  • 1 Cup Boiled sweet Corn
  • 2-3 Tbs Mayonnaise
  • 1/4 Tbs Black Pepper
  • 1/4 Tbs Salt

Procedure

Many of us get bored while making a proper sandwich for breakfast. Actually it takes a lot time to slice up veggies, like chopping down capsicum, tomato, onion, cucumber, potato, etc. For such people we came up with easy sandwich recipe for you. You just want some corn, bread, and spoonful of mayonnaise.
For making filling of corn mayo sandwich, we need bowl of boiled corns. Add spoonful of mayo into it, for enhancing some taste you can add Italian herbs or mixed herbs into it. For making it little bit spicy add some chili flakes. Add some salt to taste. Mix all together. Now you can use any one of method for toasting bread. You may toast bread first and then add corn mayo filling in between teo bread slices or you can add filling first and then toast for minutes. Don’t forget to spread butter on top of bread slice. Once your bread get toasted you can sliced your sandwich into pieces. That’s it. And your wonderful sandwich is ready for you.
