Diwali Special Chiwda | Poha Chiwda |Puffed Rice Snack

Chivda is a popular Indian snack mix made with puffed rice also known as Poha, spices, curry leaves and nuts. It is eaten all-round the year as a tea time snack. Chivda is made for festival like Diwali, Holi,etc. However Chivda mix can be made from different varieties of poha like flattened rice, puffed rice, and many more.

In this recipe we made instant poha chivda using ready made chivda masala. It is easy way to make chivda for beginners. Also it is made with easily available spices.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 Cup Dagdi Poha (Puffed Poha)
  • 1/4 Cup Roasted Chana Dal
  • 1/4 Cup Peanuts
  • 15-20 Curry Leaves
  • 5-6 Chopped Green Chillies
  • 1/2 Piece of Sliced Coconut
  • 2 Tbs Chivda Masala
  • 3 Tbs Oil
  • 1/4 Tbs Turmeric
  • Salt to Taste

Procedure

Everyone want something to eat in free time or while taking tea in evening. Chiwda is perfect snack which is made with puffed rice also called as poha , some peanuts, roasted dal, crispy curry leaves, different spices. When all this ingredients get mixed together we get a perfect chiwda to eat. Here I mentioned how usually I make chiwda at home.
You can used any kind of poha for making chiwda but I prefer dagdi poha, it’s a variety of puffed poha which have a crispy texture. First roast poha on low flame until it turns crispy. Now cut some slices of coconut and then roast some peanuts, chana dal(I used roasted chana dal here), some cashews together. Once all dry ingredients get roasted, add some oil in pan add chopped green chillies into it, fry the chillies until they turn crispy and dark in colour, then take out them and add 20-25 curry leaves. Repeat the same process. Now add 3-4 Tbs of oil in pan put a pinch of hing for taste, add some turmeric for colouring, and now add chiwda masala in oil. Give it a good stir. Fry the masala for 5 seconds now add all roasted ingredients into pan. Add poha into it and mix it well.
Your tea time snack is ready to eat.
