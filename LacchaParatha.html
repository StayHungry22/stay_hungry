Lachha Paratha Recipe | Paratha | Indian bread Recipe

Lachha paratha basically, a multi layered Indian flat bread prepared with wheat and plain flour. lachha paratha is a popular paratha recipe prepared in ease. It is very similar to Kerala paratha, however, taste different. There are many ways of preparing lachha paratha in India. It can be served with any gravy type sabji, palak paneer, aaloo gobi, malai kofta, etc.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup All-purpose flour
  • Water to knead dough
  • 1/2 Cup Oil
  • 1/4 Tbs Sugar
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

Lachha paratha is an easy paratha to make. To get these layers the dough is folded and pleated many times. There are some techniques to get the multiple layers. I am sharing the method which I use often. Take 1 Cup of all-purpose flour, put 1/2 Tbs salt, 1/4 Tbs sugar, and 2 Tbs oil. Mix all together. Mix oil nicely so that flour gets bind easily. Now add water to flour. Knead a soft dough of it. Knead dough for 8-9 Minutes so it gets perfect texture, and you can easily roll your paratha’s. After that keep aside to rest for 20 minutes.
After the dough has rested, knead dough for 2 more minutes. This process leads to make parathas softer. Now make medium sized balls of dough. Start rolling dough using dry flour. As we use all-purpose flour, it gets stretched more. So, roll the roti as thin you can. You can use hands for stretching the roti. Once it rolls to thin sheet, apply some oil through spoon or brush. Take knife and cut sheet vertically. When all sheet gets cut, collect all those strips together and roll it around your palm, and make circle of it. Now again roll it using your hand. Don’t put to much pressure on dough, otherwise the layers won’t be visible. Roll paratha to medium sized and the cook it on medium flame. Cook both sides nicely, put some oil in between, so it will get crispy and flaky layers. Repeat the same procedure for all. While serving, you can also squish the paratha in your palms (take care as they will be hot) so that the layers are visible and then serve.
You can enjoy lachha paratha with tea, or with butter on it, otherwise with some gravy-based dish.
