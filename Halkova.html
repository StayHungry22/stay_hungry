Halkova Recipe | 90's kid favourite sweet | Barfi |Sweet dish

halkova recipe – 90’s kids favourite sweet | palkova barfi | maida barfi is an easy and simple sweet snack recipe made with a combination of plain flour, ghee and sugar. It is indeed one of the favourite dessert snacks for many 90’s kids and perhaps even now. it is very simple and easy to prepare and hardly takes few minutes to prepare and can be easily served for any occasions, celebrations or ritual.

There has been several snacks and dessert recipes that were super popular in the 90’s era. gradually it has lost its charm and perhaps not liked much with the new generation and hence not much appreciated. one such 90’s favourite dessert snack is halkova recipe or also known as palkova recipe known for its crisp taste and mouth-melting texture.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup Ghee
  • 1 Cup Maida
  • 3/4 Cup Powdered Sugar
  • 1/2 Tbs Elaichi Powder
  • Almonds for Garnishing

Procedure

Add ghee in kadhai, once ghee get melted add Plain flour in ghee.Turn gas on medium flame , stir mixture continuously. Roast the plain flour nicely, until its raw taste gone. once it completely rosted, take out in bowl. Let it cool down slightly.
Add powdered sugar according to your sweetness, add 1 Tablespoon of cardamom powder. Once everything get well combined, add the barfi mixture in container. set the mixture for 2-3 hours in fridge. You will get perfect texture of barfi. Once it get set cut into square pieces. Your halkova barfi is ready to serve.
