Chicken Masala Recipe | Chicken Gravy | Non-Vegetarian Dish

Learn to make Chicken masala Which is favorite item of all chicken lover. This is one of the most simple, home style Indian chicken recipe for beginners.So any one can cook it very easily.
This chicken masala goes great with parathas, rice, any type of roti, naan. There are many ways a masala chicken recipe is made across India. Each region has their own way of making it with locally available taste enhancers like coconut, cashews, sesame seeds, poppy seeds etc.

But I shared a simple and delicious way of making semi dry chicken masala recipe with you. No need to add cream or yogurt for giving a creamy texture. Simply by adding following ingredients you’ll get a perfect gravy base to your dish.

Ingredients

-For Marination:

  • 1 Tbs Red chilli powder
  • 1/2 Tbs Turmeric
  • 1/4 Tbs Black papper
  • 1 Tbs Ginger garlic paste

-For Cooking:

  • 1 Tbs Oil
  • 5-6 Garlic cloves
  • 1 inch Ginger
  • small piece Coconut
  • 1 Kashmiri red chilli
  • 1 Tomato
  • 2 Onions
  • 1/2 Tbs Cumin (Jeera)
  • Cinnamon, Bay Leaf, Black pappers, Cloves
  • 1/4 Tbs Hing (Asafetida)
  • 1 Tbs Red chilli powder
  • 1/2 Tbs Turmeric
  • 1/2 Tbs Garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 Tbs Chicken masala
  • 1/2 Tbs Kashmiri red chilli powder
  • Coriander

Procedure

As you have eaten various versions of chicken dishes in many hotels, restaurants, at home or in native places, anywhere you go, you will get a lilltle bit difference in that recipe. Some hotels use curd base curries to give a creamy texture, in south region they use coconut milk to give it a rich Taste. Here I shared a simple, authentic, chicken masala recipe with you. I used to make it chicken gravy in such a way, and it turns mouth-watering at end.
First you need fresh chicken meat, clean it and then add spices like red chilli powder, turmeric, garam masala, half spoon of cumin powder, black pepper powder, along with spoonful of ginger-garlic paste.gentelly apply all the spices on meat. And keep it for 30minutes to 1 hour. So that meat get coated in spices properly.
For preparing a thick gravy you need a perfect rich base for it. For this chopped some onions and tomatoes. Put some oil in pan add aliced onions and tomatoes, add 5-6 garlic cloves, inch of ginger piece, 2-3 kashmiri chillies, put some pieces of dry coconuts, 1/2 spoon of cumin and roast it for 4-5 minutes. Once it get soften cool it down, and then grind the mixture into smooth paste.
Once gravy base get ready. Put some oil in pan gain, add pinch of Asafoetida(Hing),1/2 Tbs jeera, piece of cinnamon, 2 bay leaves, 2-3 Cloves, 2-3 peppercorns. Roast until aroma somes from it. Then add prepared gravy, masala into it. Fry it for 5 minutes. It starts seprating oil from it. Add salt to taste. Then add marinated Chicken into it. Add some water as you required according to gravy. Keep flame medium and cook for 10-15 minutes. Garnish with freshly chopped coriander. And you are good to go. Your perfect masala chicken is ready!
