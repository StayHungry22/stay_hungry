Fenugreek Leaves Paratha | Methi Paratha | Paratha Recipe

Methi paratha is a healthy and tasty whole wheat flatbread made with fenugreek leaves. These fenugreek flatbreads are a healthy alternative to plain paratha and packed with more flavours and nutrition.

Remarkable punch in the paratha is that of Garlic. The Fenugreek and garlic blend so well with each other in these parathas. So, you have both the awesome flavours of fenugreek and garlic when you bite into the paratha.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 Cup wheat flour
  • 1/2 Cup Methi / Fenugreek leaves.
  • 2 Tbs Gram flour
  • 5-6 Green Chillies
  • 1 piece of ginger
  • 7-8 Garlic cloves
  • 1 Tbs Red chilli powder
  • 1/2 Tbs Turmeric
  • 1/2 Tbs Cumin
  • 1/2 Tbs Carom seeds
  • 1.5 Tbs Sesame seeds
  • 1 Tbs kasoori methi
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil

Procedure

Various versions of paratha’s are there in menu lists. Methi paratha is one of the easy and tasty versions of it. It is very simple to make within half an hour you can made it.
Take 1.5 Cup of wheat flour in big plate add 2 Tbs of besan into it. Besan or gram flour. gives binding to paratha. Then add 1/2 Tbs of Carom seeds (ajwain), 1/2 Tbs of cumin seeds, 1/2 Tbs turmeric, 1 Tbs red chilli powder, 1 Tbs kasoori methi, salt to taste, 1.5 Tbs Sesame seeds, 1 Tbs chilli-ginger paste. Mix all these together. Now add fresh washed Fenugreek leaves (methi) into it. Mash while mixing it with flour. Then add water little at time and make a medium soft dough out of it. Add 1 Tbs oil on dough and rest it for 5 minutes.
Once dough get ready take small portion of dough and make small balls. Dip it in some flour and roll it to medium-thin roti \ Paratha. Heat pan on medium flame. Cook paratha for 2 minutes evenly on both sides. Add some butter on top of it. Serve this hot with dry garlic-chilli chutney.
