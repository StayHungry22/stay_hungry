Cutlet Recipe | Easy snack recipe | Potato cutlet | Aaloo Tikki |

Cutlet is an easy and simple deep fried teatime snack, prepared with boiled potatoes and other dry spices. These cutlets are crispy with the outer layer and soft and spicy within its inner layer.

Apart from consuming as a snack, it can also be used for other purposes like patties, chats, sandwiches and even for chapati based Frankie or rolls. however, for the vegetarian and non-meat eaters, the cutlet is prepared with a vegetable or combination of veggies. One such vegetable-based cutlet recipe is aloo cutlet or also known as aloo tikki, cutlet recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 Boiled potatoes
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 Tbs ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 Tbs cumin seeds
  • 1/4 Tbs Hing (Asafoetida)
  • 1/2 Tbs cumin powder
  • 1/2 Tbs black pepper
  • 1/4 Tbs turmeric
  • 1/2 Tbs red chilli powder
  • 1.5 Tbs pav bhaji masala
  • 1/2 Cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 Tbs corn flour
  • Coriander
  • Oil

Procedure

Potato cutlets, Simple and easy to make. You can use same recipe to prepare veg burger tikki’s. Here I shared a recipe that I usually used.

Mash 4-5 Boiled potatoes, using masher or spatula. Add 1 chopped onion, 1 Tbs ginger-garlic paste, Salt to taste, 1 Tbs cumin, 1/4 Tbs Hing (Asafoetida). And other dry spices like 1/2 Tbs cumin powder,1/2 Tbs black pepper, 1/4 Tbs turmeric, 1/2 Tbs red chilli powder, 1.5 Tbs pav bhaji masala, 1/2 Cup breadcrumbs. Breadcrumbs helps in binding of mixture. Mix all ingredients together. Then add Chopped coriander into it. Mix again. It turns out dough like texture. Then brush tray or plate with some oil. Then Put prepared mixture in tray. Spread evenly. Then refrigerate it for 2-3 hours.

Once they get set. Make cut on it. And take them out from tray. Now take a bowl. Add 2 Tbs corn flour, put 3-4 Tbs water to it. Make thick slurry of flour. Take 1/2 Cup breadcrumbs in plate. Now Put some oil to fry in deep pan. Heat oil on medium flame. Take one-piece prepared cutlet, dip it in corn flour slurry, and coat it with breadcrumbs. Repeat the process for rest of cutlets. And deep fry them in oil one by one. Cook until they get crunchy and crispy from outside. They turn dark golden in colour after cooking.
Serve these Super crunchy and delicious cutlets with some sauces, or ketchup.
