Trending Wrap Recipe | Veg Tortilla Wrap | Street food Recipe

Tiktok trending tortilla wrap. It is easy, tasty and take less time to make this. To make this delicious wrap all you need is tortillas(roti), tikki, salad that include cabbage, onions, tomatoes, etc, some cheese and your favourite sauces.
put all these together in proper manner on Roti / tortilla fold it in triangular shape and toast it with some butter. Also, you can add your favourite veggies different types of tikkies on it.

Here I made aaloo tikki as stuffing and chatpata salad for Tangy taste.

Ingredients

  • 4-5 Boiled potatoes
  • 1 Cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 Cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 Tbs grated beetroot
  • 1 Tbs Pav Bhaji Masala
  • 1/2 Tbs cumin (jeera) powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 Tbs black pepper
  • 1/4 Tbs dry mango powder
  • 1/4 Tbs turmeric
  • 1/2 Tbs ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 Tbs Corn flour
  • 1 Tbs plain flour
  • 2-3 Tbs Oil
  • Finely chopped cabbage
  • Finley sliced cucumber
  • 1/2 Tbs Chat masala
  • Tortillas \ Roti \ Chapati
  • 2 Tbs Tomato ketchup
  • 1 Tbs Mayonnaise
  • Grated cheese

Procedure

Before it gets trending on social median, everyone eats it at their home. Like making Frankie’s, or by putting sauces, and some veggies in tortillas. So here I show how I preferred to make this wrap recipe.
For stuffing we made a potato based tikki. Also called as aaloo tikki. For this you need 2-3 boiled potatoes. Mash the boiled potatoes in bowl, then add 1/2 chopped onion, 1/2 Cup breadcrumbs for binding, 1 Tbs grated beetroot (For rich colour), 1 Tbs Pav Bhaji Masala, 1/2 Tbs cumin (jeera) powder, Salt to taste, 1/4 Tbs black pepper, 1/4 Tbs dry mango powder, 1/4 Tbs turmeric, 1/2 Tbs ginger-garlic paste. Mix all these together. And prepare tikkis of it. I made triangular Tikkis so it can fit in tortillas easily. Once tikkis get ready. Take a bowl add 1 Tbs of all-purpose flour, and corn flour into it. Add some water to it and make slurry. Now Take tikki, dip into slurry, after that coat it with breadcrumbs. So, it gets a coating over it. Repeat procedure for rest of tikkis. Now put some oil in deep pan, Heat oil on medium flame, once it gets heated, fry tikkis one by one. Fry until they turn crispy, and golden in colour.
Now Start preparing the salad for wrap. For that take 3 Tbs Finely chopped cabbage, 3 Tbs finely chopped onion, 3 Tbs finely sliced cucumber, Salt to taste, 1/2 Tbs Chat masala. Mix altogether. And your salad is ready. Grate some cheese to put it on wrap. Take 2 Tbs ketchup, 1 Tbs red chilli sauce, 1 Tbs mayonnaise mix all sauces together, and our mixed sauce is ready.
Now everything is ready with us, we have to assemble them in a manner.

First take a tortilla or roti, give a slit using knife on downside, so it become easy to fold the wrap. Now put tikki one side, put mixed sauce on one side. Put some prepared salad on one side, and grated cheese on last side. So, every corner of tortilla is now filled with some ingredients. Now fold the tortilla side one on another. Like that complete the rest of wraps. And you are good to go with trendy Tortilla wrap.
