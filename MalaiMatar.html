Malai Matar | Gravy sabji | Restaurant style curry

Malai Matar is special Sabji or Menu dish of Indian Cuisine. It is perfect combination with Roti / parathas or Rice. Malai Matar Consist a Thick gravy which insist you to lick your fingers again and again. It can be made in 30-35 minutes. You just have to prepare a gravy base and you are ready to add some fresh veggies like green peas, cauliflower, capsicum and others. I made this by using seasonal vegetables like green peas & Cauliflower. Using same gravy base, you can make other vegetable dishes too.

Ingredients

  • 1 bowl of cauliflower
  • 1 Cup Boiled or Frozen Green peas
  • 2 sliced Onion
  • 1 Chopped Tomato
  • 1 inch Ginger piece
  • 5-6 Garlic cloves
  • 2 Kashmiri Mirch
  • 7-8 cashew nuts
  • 1 Tbs Poopy seed
  • 2-3 Tbs Oil
  • 1Tbs Jeera
  • 1 Tbs Red Chili powder
  • 1/2 Tbs Turmeric
  • 1/2 Tbs Garam Masala
  • Salt to Taste
  • 1 Tbs Kasoori methi
  • 1 Cup water
  • Coriander for garnishing

Procedure

It is one of the dishes that serves in many hotels along with naan, rotis and rice. Many of its versions are available. Here I show how to make it in easy and in better way. Add 2 Tbs oil in pan, add 2 sliced onions into it, then add 1 sliced tomato, chopped pieces of ginger, 5-6 garlic cloves, add 1/2 Tbs of salt. Cook for 2 minutes. Then let it cool down. Then grind it to a fine paste. Now take 5-6 Cashew nuts and 1 Tbs of poppy seeds grind it to fine paste using some water.
Add some oil in pan, fry the cauliflower pieces little bit, to take out its raw taste. Then Boil a cup of water and add green peas into it. Boil for 5-6 Minutes. You can skip this step if you are using frozen green peas.
Now to prepare dish add 2-3 Tbs oil in pan, add ½ Tbs cumin seeds. Once it gets roasted add tomato-onion paste into it. Cook it for 2 minutes then add dry spices like 1 Tbs red chilli powder, 1/2 Tbs turmeric, 1/2 Tbs garam masala, 1 Tbs of kasoori methi. Get all mix together. Then add cashew paste into gravy. It gives a rich taste to gravy. Cook for a minute then add a cup of water. Let it boil. Add boiled peas and fried cauliflower into gravy. Stir gravy continuously. Once gravy get cooked garnish it with freshly chopped coriander. And your hotel style malai matar sabji is ready.
